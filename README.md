```markdown
# Domain Knowledge: E-Commerce

## 1. Business Models in E-Commerce
- **B2C (Business to Consumer)**: Contoh: Amazon, Shopee
- **B2B (Business to Business)**: Contoh: Alibaba, Grainger
- **C2C (Consumer to Consumer)**: Contoh: eBay, OLX
- **D2C (Direct to Consumer)**: Brand menjual langsung ke konsumen via website sendiri
- **Dropshipping**: Menjual produk tanpa menyimpan stok

## 2. Digital Marketing & SEO
- Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
- Pay-per-click (PPC) Ads (Google Ads)
- Social Media Marketing (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
- Email Marketing & Customer Retention
- Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

## 3. E-Commerce Platforms & Tools
- **Platform**: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce
- **Marketplace**: Amazon, Tokopedia, Lazada
- **Tools**: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Mailchimp, Klaviyo

## 4. User Experience (UX) & Design
- Desain responsif mobile
- Kecepatan loading halaman
- Deskripsi produk jelas & gambar berkualitas tinggi
- Proses checkout sederhana
- Elemen kepercayaan (review, rating, badge keamanan)

## 5. Payment Systems
- Payment Gateway: PayPal, Stripe, Midtrans, Xendit
- Metode pembayaran: Kartu kredit, transfer bank, e-wallet (OVO, GoPay)
- Opsi cicilan: Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)

## 6. Logistics & Fulfillment
- Manajemen inventaris
- Pengiriman & tracking
- Penanganan retur dan refund
- Integrasi dengan 3PL (Third Party Logistics)

## 7. Customer Service
- Live chat, email support, chatbot
- CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- Penanganan keluhan & feedback

## 8. Legal & Compliance
- Perlindungan data (GDPR, PDPA)
- Hukum perlindungan konsumen
- Kepatuhan pajak (PPN, sales tax)
- Term & Conditions, Privacy Policy

## 9. Analytics & Performance Monitoring
- KPI penting: 
  - Conversion Rate
  - Average Order Value (AOV)
  - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  - Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Tools: Google Analytics, Hotjar, Mixpanel

## 10. Trends & Innovations
- AI & Personalization
- AR/VR untuk preview produk
- Voice commerce
- E-commerce berkelanjutan & etis
```
